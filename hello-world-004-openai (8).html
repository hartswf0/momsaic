1. DRIVING & COMBAT

  • Left Joystick (Mobile) / WASD (PC): Steer and accelerate. Note: Steering is Camera-Relative! Up is always away from the camera.
  • JUMP (Spacebar): Leap into the air. Use at the lip of a ramp for massive air.
  • BOOST (Shift): Engage thrusters for 150% torque to climb steep quarterpipes.
  • FIRE (F): Shoot high-speed lasers. Lasers damage and demolish rival rigs as well as arena parts.
  • RAMMING: Hit another rig at speed to crush its integrity. Faster closing speed causes heavier damage.

2. CAMERA MODES

Tap the CAR / FREE / BALL button in the WORLD toolbar to cycle views:

  • CAR / BALL: Rigidly locks focus to the moving object.
  • ACTION: Dynamically centers the view between the car and the ball.
  • FREE: Detaches camera for stable CAD building.

Lost? Tap the red HOME button at the top left to instantly reset the car and camera.

3. AI AGENT BUILDING

Open the AGENT tab to command the AI via Text or Voice.

  • The AI can build using Beams, Panels, Blocks, Ramps, and Quarterpipes.
  • Always ask the AI to use "Ramps" or "Quarterpipes" if you want to jump.
  • Example Prompts: "Build a halfpipe", "Generate a chair", "Make a jump gap".
  • When the Blueprint Draft appears, use the Inspector HUD at the top to Nudge it into place before hitting COMMIT.

3B. API KEYS & ROOM AI

  • Open AGENT → API to use the official OpenAI Responses API directly, or choose another provider/custom endpoint.
  • Keys are stored in your browser only. Use a temporary or restricted key because browser users can inspect client-side credentials.
  • While AI builds, the rig’s rear light and spoiler illuminate from left to right as the progress meter.
  • Enable HOST AI FOR ROOM if you want other players to use your AI during online play. Their prompt is relayed to your browser; your browser calls the API; only the resulting blueprint returns to the room.
  • Do not paste a billing-critical production API key into a shared/public machine. Use a temporary or capped key for play sessions.

3C. MULTIPLAYER ROOMS

  • Open MULTI → ROOMS, choose HOST + QR, then let other players scan the code. Scanned links join automatically.
  • Rooms sync live player cars, host ball state, arena snapshots, and AI requests over the Cloudflare Worker.
  • Last arena edit wins. If two people build at the same moment, the newest snapshot becomes the room state.
  • Combat is target-authoritative: each player owns their own integrity, receives hit events, breaks apart, and respawns.

3D. BOTS, BUILDERS & FORTS

  • Open BOTS → ADD to deploy Raider, Builder, or Hybrid rigs. Offline play and the multiplayer host own bot simulation.
  • API ARCHITECT lets a bot occasionally ask the configured AI for a compact fort plan. Driving and fighting never depend on the model.
  • With or without API access, every fort is compiled through a vehicle-safe grammar: a wide gate, an empty turning court, no low roof, no central obstacles, and ramps outside the driving lane.
  • The API chooses a doctrine and proportions. Local code owns the geometry, so a model cannot dump overlapping parts into the arena.
  • Bot rear lights and spoilers show planning/construction progress. Fort pieces appear one at a time and remain fully destructible.
  • Use FORT ALL to issue an immediate construction order.

4. MANUAL CAD & SCENE GRAPH

  • Open the GRAPH (World tab) to see a list of everything in the arena. Tap a name to select it.
  • Use the massive 3D Gizmo to drag objects, OR use the PRECISE NUDGE (+ / -) buttons in the top Inspector for perfect snapping.
  • Tap CLONE to duplicate entire assemblies instantly.

5. KINEMATICS & ANIMATION

Make your arenas alive! Select any object or AI assembly and tap the ANIMATE tab in the Inspector.

  • SPIN: Endless rotation (Great for windmills and revolving doors).
  • OSCILLATE: Back-and-forth movement (Great for elevators and moving platforms).
  • FLIPPER: Snappy rotational kick (Great for pinball flippers and catapults).
